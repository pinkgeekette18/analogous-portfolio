About Me

I am passionate about the web.

I've made my home base in Tampa, Florida, with my small growing family. My journey into tech started with a degree in graphic design. As I started sharing my art work online, I was fascinated with how easily I could get my creations out there. I was hooked and wanted to learn how to build my own site to fit my own specific needs.

That curiosity then opened a door that could never be shut. When I learned how to build my first website, I realized I found something that gave me the freedom & versatility I was looking for in my work. Now I've made a full switch to front-end development, where I can use my organization skills and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code.

Can't wait to work with you!
