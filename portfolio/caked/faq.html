What is a Cake Pop?

A Cake Pop is all the yummy goodness of a cupcake hand rolled into a ball coverd in icing, and dipped in chocolate, and served on a stick. Portable, customizable, and completely mess free. Cake Pops are also great because they come in a wide variety of flavors and toppings! Try up to three different flavors per order! Cake Pops also have a shelf life much longer than any regular old cake or cupcake and will last well over a week!

What is the best method to store cakepops and mini cupcakes?

It’s really what you prefer. Some prefer to eat them at room temperature; others prefer to keep them slightly chilled in the refrigerator. Because of the hard chocolate coating, the Cake Pops have a much longer shelf life than a regular cupcake, and will last over a week. For the longest shelf life the refrigerator is probable the best.
