"What is a Cake Pop?" you ask?

Cake Pops are the newest and trendiest dessert around. Imagine all the yumminess of a cupcake and icing hand rolled into a ball, dipped in chocolate and served on a stick. Cake Pops are portable, customizable and completely mess free. Have just one for a little bite of decadence or have as many as you want in an endless variety of flavors! Cake Pops are also great for large parties and corporate events in our custom Cake Pop Cake displays. Buy your Cake Pops today!
